Xiaomi Pad 6 TWRP Flashing Guide

Prerequisites

  • A Windows PC
  • ADB and Fastboot installed
  • Download TWRP for Pad 6
  • Stock boot image (Download from your device's official firmware page)
  • USB cable
  • Backup your data before proceeding
  • Charge your Pad 6 to at least 60%
  • USB OTG adapter and a mouse (for initial navigation in TWRP)

Important Notes

  • Touch Issue Workaround: Touch functionality in TWRP will work after you put the tablet to sleep and wake it up again. Use the power button to put the device to sleep, then press it again to wake up the tablet, and touch will start working.
  • Custom ROM Compatibility: TWRP does not support custom ROMs on this device due to kernel version incompatibilities. The kernel in custom ROMs is newer than MIUI/HyperOS, and this breaks compatibility with TWRP.

1. Enter Fastboot Mode

  1. Power off your Pad 6.
  2. Press and hold Volume Down + Power until you see the Fastboot logo.
  3. Connect the device to your PC using a USB cable.

2. Flash TWRP

  1. Open a command prompt on your PC and navigate to the folder where ADB/Fastboot is installed.
  2. Type the following command to ensure Fastboot recognizes your device: 
    fastboot devices
  3. If your device is listed, proceed by typing the following command to flash TWRP: 
    fastboot flash boot twrp.img

3. Reboot to TWRP

  1. Once TWRP is flashed, type the following command to boot into TWRP: 
    fastboot reboot recovery

4. Initial Navigation in TWRP Using a Mouse

At first, touch may not work in TWRP. You will need to use a USB OTG adapter and plug in a mouse for initial navigation.

Touch will start working after you put the tablet to sleep and wake it up.

5. Install Stock Boot Image

  1. In TWRP, go to Install and select your stockboot.img file.
  2. Flash the boot image to the Boot partition to ensure your device boots properly.

6. Flash TWRP to Ramdisk

  1. After flashing the stock boot image, go to Advanced.
  2. Select Flash to Ramdisk, then choose the twrp.img file to make TWRP permanent.

7. Reboot the System

  1. Once everything is done, go to Reboot and select System.
  2. Your Xiaomi Pad 6 should now boot with TWRP installed, and you can access it in the future by booting into recovery.
